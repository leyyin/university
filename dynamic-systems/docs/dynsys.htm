Grading Policy

 

·        20% two seminar tests in Seminar 3 and Seminar 6

·        15%  lab reports (each lab) and final Lab Test in Lab 7

·        55% final (written) exam

·        10% granted

 

 

                    Minimal requirements in order to participate to the final exam 

·        It is compulsory to participate to at least 6 of the 7 Labs and at least to 5 of the 7 Seminars.

 

                     Normally, the student has to attend the Labs and Seminars as scheduled for his group.

                     In the case he missed one of such date he can go with another group. 

                    

 

                     Minimal requirements in order to pass the final exam

·        To obtain at least 5 points (from the 10) at the Lab Test

·        To obtain at least 2.0 points (from the 5.5) at the final written exam

·        To obtain at least 4.5 points (from the 10)
