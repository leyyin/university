Assignments (homeworks)

Homeworks are discussed during lecture classes.
They consist of short problems related to what was discussed in class.
They should be delivered at specified seminar class.

  1. List with internal cursor
    • ADT
    • subalg. that prints all the element in a list
      (specification, pseudocode)
    Delivery: seminar no.2
  2. Map or MultiMap
    • ADT
    • you can use iterator; if so, please specify also Iterator (ADT)
    Delivery: seminar no.3
  3. Vector: analize capacity increment strategies
    • Show that inserting a series of elements into a vector is a quadratic time operation, when increasing its capacity with fixed constant
      (study effects of incrementing the current capacity with constant 4)
    • follow the steps discussed during lecture class
      (we saw that inserting a series of elements into a vector is a linear time operation, when increasing its capacity proportional to the current capacity ; we studied effects of doubling the current capacity)
    Delivery: seminar no.4
  4. Postorder tree traversal. Show how the recursive subalg. works. Use a concrete example.
    • give the subalg.
    • ilustrate (recursive) calls and results
    Delivery: seminar no.5
  5. Binary search tree
    • add, remove, inorder traversal - with a representation without a link to parent
    • or: add, inorder traversal
      right rotate
    (specification, pseudocode)
    Delivery: seminar no.6
    One more:
  • Robot in a maze: find an optimum solution.
    • breadth first search
    • use STL stack and queue
    • The program: printed or in electronic format
    Delivery: seminar no.5
