MLE022: Data Structures and Algorithms

Grading details

The final grade will be computed as follows:
  • 20% Project
  • 20% Seminar activity
  • 60% Written exam

Minimal passing criteria

  • minimum grade of 5 at the written exam
  • minimum grade of 5 for seminar activity
  • minimum grade of 5 for the final computed grade

Seminar activity

Seminar activity (grading) will be evaluated based on the solutions given for 6 set of problems
(homeworks or seminar assignements).
  • Problems will be stated during lecture classes together with indication of the seminar class when should be delivered.
  • Each of them will consist of 20% of the seminar activity grade.
  • The solution will be delivered to the seminar instructor at the beginning of the seminar activity. No late delivery will be accepted.
  • Seminar assignments that were not presented during seminars, can be delivered only to satisfy the minimality criterion (at the end of the activity).

Project grading

A detailed presentation of project grading and project delivery schedule can be found in section Project
Notice that there is one-time only project delivery!

The reexamination session

In the reexamination session the same grading algorithm will apply.
  • seminar assignments can be delivered only to satisfy the minimality criterion
